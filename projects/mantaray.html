Laser Cut Manta Ray

Final Product

This Manta Ray is a laser cut prompt piece made using the softwares Sketchup and Rhino and cut out of wood using a laser cutter. Each piece was designed to slide perfectly into place creating a model that appears 3D in form with on only 2D materials.
