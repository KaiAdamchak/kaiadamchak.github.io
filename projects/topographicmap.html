Topographic Map of Colorado

Final Product

Using laser cut plywood this piece is a 3D recreation of a topographic map of the state that I've lived my whole life in.

Process

Tools Used

Illustrator

Laser Cutter

Materials

Plywood Panel

Problems

When it came the design process this piece was a lot of fun and it gave me a chance to get some practice with the pen tool in Illustrator.

However, the material I was given for this project did not cut well as all with the laser cutter and as a result I had a lot of issues during the process of this piece. I completely destroyed my first piece of wood after trying to cut it amost 3 times. I then got another piece of wood and turned down the laser cutters speed so that it cut most of the way through eventually. Then the rectangles I used to stack the pieces had a lot of problems resulting in all the connections heavily relying on hot glue. Eventually I do like the end product of the piece the process was just a lot of problem solving rather than a plan that wokred out well.
